## Description (4,000 characters)
Tired of endless scrolling and algorithmic rabbit holes? Abridged rethinks how you consume local news.

Instead of infinite feeds designed to keep you scrolling, Abridged delivers a finite, curated daily edition of Pittsburgh's most important stories. Read less, understand more.

**Features:**
- **Daily Curated Edition:** Handpicked stories from Pittsburgh's best news sources, delivered fresh each day
- **Distraction-Free Reading:** Clean, typography-focused interface—no ads, no tracking, no algorithmic manipulation
- **Classic Reading Mode:** Calm, deliberate reading experience with full story context
- **RSVP Speed Reading (Coming Soon):** Rapidly consume stories using Rapid Serial Visual Presentation technology for high-speed information ingestion
- **Local Focus:** Stay connected to Pittsburgh—the neighborhoods, events, and stories that matter to your community
- **Offline Reading:** Save stories to read later, even without internet
- **Simple Navigation:** Organized by news categories for easy discovery

**Why Abridged?**
Modern news apps are built to maximize engagement—not to serve you well. They gamify consumption, exploit psychological triggers, and bury important stories under noise.

Abridged is different. We believe news should be:
- **Finite:** A daily digest you can actually finish
- **Focused:** On your community, not viral trends
- **Calm:** Designed for thoughtful reading, not addictive scrolling
- **Accessible:** Clear typography and intuitive design for all readers

Perfect for Pittsburgh locals who want to stay informed about their city without the digital overwhelm.

**Coming Soon:**
- RSVP speed reading mode for rapid story consumption
- Customizable news preferences and topics
- Multi-city support
- Enhanced sharing and annotation features

Get back to reading what matters. Download Abridged today.
