# Features Summary — Abridged v1.0.0

**Build:** 1.0.0
**Release Date:** January 2026
**Platform:** iOS (TestFlight Beta)

---

## Current Features (v1.0.0)

### 📰 **Home Feed**
Browse the latest news articles curated for Pittsburgh. Each article displays:
- Headline and summary
- Source and timestamp
- Category (Top, Local, Politics, etc.)
- Tap to read the full story

### 📑 **News Digest**
A daily digest view that summarizes today's top stories. Perfect for quick catch-ups during your commute or lunch break.

### 🔖 **Saved Articles**
Bookmark articles to read later. All saved articles are stored locally on your device and sync across app sessions.

### ⚙️ **Settings**
Customize your reading experience:
- **Reading Preferences:** Font size, line spacing, reader mode options
- **Digest Settings:** Customize your daily digest
- **Theme & Colors:** Light mode (dark mode coming soon)
- **Feedback:** Report bugs or send suggestions directly to the team
- **About:** View app version and contact info

### 🎨 **Design System**
- Clean, minimal interface focused on reading
- Consistent typography and spacing
- Touch-friendly buttons and navigation
- No ads, no infinite scroll, no distracting notifications

## Design Principles

Every feature in Abridged follows these core principles:

1. **Calm First:** No aggressive notifications, no infinite scrolls, no engagement manipulation.
2. **Finite Editions:** There's always a "done" state. Read today's news, then move on.
3. **Respectful Design:** User control, no dark patterns, transparent about data.
4. **Accessibility:** Works for everyone, regardless of ability.
5. **Local Focus:** Curated for Pittsburgh and its communities.
